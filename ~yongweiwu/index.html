Yongwei Wu

Professor, Vice Department Head, IEEE Fellow
Department of Computer Science & Technology
Tsinghua University

Address: FIT 3-110, Tsinghua University, Beijing, P. R. China, 100084

Email: wuyw(at)tsinghua.edu.cn

[ Education and Career | Professional Services | Awards | Publications | Teaching ]

My research interests mainly focus on Runtime Environment, Data Storage/Management, Virtualization, Resource Management, Distributed Scheduling, Performance Analysis, Reliability/Fault Tolerant in the Parallel and Distributed Systems. I also do some research on Machine Learning and Data Mining.
